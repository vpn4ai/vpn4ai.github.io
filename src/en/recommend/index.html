Can a VPN guarantee 100% availability?

No. Network routing, congestion, and provider load can all affect connectivity. A better approach is choosing a provider with stronger redundancy and recovery behavior over time.

Why does it work sometimes but not others?

This is often caused by routing changes, node load, protocol compatibility, or local network conditions. Providers with more nodes and mature clients tend to recover more easily.

Are free VPNs reliable?

Many free VPNs have limitations in stability and privacy transparency. For longâ€‘term use or workâ€‘related scenarios, relying on free options is usually not recommended.
