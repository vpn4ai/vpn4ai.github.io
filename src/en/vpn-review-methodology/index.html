How we review VPN services

  • Long-term stability: we focus on consistency over time, not a single short test.
  • Node coverage & redundancy: more options matter when some nodes perform poorly.
  • Protocol & compatibility: mature protocols and solid clients reduce friction.
  • Privacy policy & transparency: clear logging policy and security practices.
  • User experience: onboarding, apps, kill switch, and recovery behavior.
  • Cost over time: long-term pricing and plan value.

Network conditions vary by region and can change over time. No service can guarantee 100% availability in all conditions.
